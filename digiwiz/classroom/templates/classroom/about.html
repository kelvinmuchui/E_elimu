DigiWiz: The Open-source Learning Platform

Importance of Education

Education serves as the means to bring about the desired change in society, to develop a generation of virtuous individuals and thus contribute to the development of good human beings. The importance of education to youths manifests itself in terms of the need to cultivate the youths of society into mature individuals.

United Nation's Sustainable Development Goal

By 2030, ensure that all girls and boys have access to quality early childhood development, care and pre-primary education so that they are ready for primary education

The Solution: DigiWiz

In order to support the United Nations’ sustainable development goal, our group will develop DigiWiz, an open-source learning platform. The platform’s goal is to help people learn certain subjects for free with the help of registered instructors. These instructors will have the freedom to add courses/topics that they want to share with those who are willing to learn. For it to be a success, the contents of the instructors’ created courses will be monitored by the system admin to prevent any duplication of courses and possible fraud.
